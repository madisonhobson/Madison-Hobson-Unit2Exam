Microsofts Computer Development


  • April 4, 1975: Microsoft Founded


    • Bill Gates and Paul Allen founded Microsoft on April 4, 1975, in Albuquerque, New Mexico.


  • November 22, 2005: Xbox 360 Launch


    • Microsoft launched the Xbox 360, a highly successful gaming console that introduced features like Xbox Live, high-definition gaming, and a wireless controller.


  • October 21, 2010: Windows Phone 7 Release


    • Microsoft released Windows Phone 7, a mobile operating system designed to compete with iOS and Android. It featured a new interface called Metro and integration with Microsoft services.


  • November 22, 2013: Xbox One Launch


    • The Xbox One was released, succeeding the Xbox 360. It included improved hardware, a Kinect sensor, and a focus on multimedia and entertainment features.


  • February 24, 2019: HoloLens 2 Announcement


    • Microsoft announced the HoloLens 2, an augmented reality headset that offered enhanced features over its predecessor. It featured a more significant field of view, improved gesture controls, and a more comfortable design.
